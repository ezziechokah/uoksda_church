ABOUT US

Mission

The University of Kabianga Seventh-Day Adventist Church (UOKSDA) is an active faith community within the University of Kabianga. We are located in Kabianga, Kericho County, Kenya. The church serves as a spiritual hub for students, faculty, and the surrounding community (Matthew 5:14), offering worship services, prayer meetings, and various religious activities which plays a role in spiritual growth and gives hope of the coming King. The church is affiliated with the Seventh-Day Adventist Church, a global Christian denomination emphasizing The Three Angels' Message (Revelation 14); Sabbath observance, healthful living, and Bible-based teachings... Keeping all the commandments of God (Exodus 20:1-17, Deuteronomy 5:16-22) and have the testimony of Jesus Christ, which is the spirit of prophecy (Revelation 12:17, 19:10). It plays a vital role in the spiritual life of students at the University of Kabianga, offering guidance and fellowship.

Worship & Activities

The church is dedicated to spiritual growth, evangelism, community service and outreach activities. We organize:

  • Weekly Sabbath services with sermons, Bible study, and worship, daily prayer meetings and devotions
  • Music and Choir (including ministry choirs) performances that enhance the worship experience.
  • Special weeks like the Prophecy, Health, Music, Family and Revival Weeks, focusing on evangelism and faith-building (Baptism after the special weeks). we conduct special visitations, both to our members and the brethren from the other faiths, with the greatest aim to show the love of Christ.
  • Farewell and Staff Sabbaths to celebrate graduating students, and fellowship with the older staff respectively.
  • Mission (Evangelism) at the end of every semester where we visit different places for the sake of the gospel(Matthew 28:19, Mark 16:15, Romans 10:14)
